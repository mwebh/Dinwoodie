Welcome to Dinwoodie Motors Ltd.


Located on Chestnut Lane, Stroud. Dinwoodie Motors Ltd specialises in MOT's, servicing, repairs and more. Previously known as Steve Beavis MOT Centre. Dinwoodie Motors is now proud to provide excellent levels of service to Stroud and the surrounding areas.

Services we offer

MOT's

We offer MOT services for class 4, 5 & 7 vehicles

Servicing

We have the facilities for servicing many different vehicle types

Diagnostics

Not sure what's wrong? We have the ability to diagnose any issues with your vehicle

Repairs

We can help if you're in need of repair, call to speak to a member of the team

Have a question about something else? Give us a call
